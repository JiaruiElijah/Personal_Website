Recur Click here to view.

Recur is a first-person narrative game with an innovative gameplay that combines roguelike deck-building combat and Tetris. The player is trapped in a recurring space of an art museum. You will have to fight against the roaming pieces of art and sculptures in the gallery. Capture the wandering enemies with your camera, stack different shapes of blocks to form eliminable rows, and execute a variety of skills to defeat the enemies.
